Born in the
Shadow of Deomali.

Our journey begins in the Koraput valley, where the peaks of Deomali pierce the clouds. Here, the soil is rich, volcanic, and untouched. We work directly with smallholder farmers who have cultivated these lands for generations.

Every bean is hand-picked at the peak of ripeness and sun-dried on raised beds. This isn't just about coffee; it's about preserving a micro-climate and a heritage of bold, uncompromising flavor.
